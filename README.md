# SUBNET LIFT – Vertical Order v2.1

**SUBNET LIFT** is a high-fidelity, standalone elevator logistics simulation set in a corporate dystopia cyberpunk future. v2.1 introduces an immersive atmospheric layer with cinematic office environments and low-poly spirit character sprites.

## 🏗️ Core Game Mechanics
Manage a fleet of elevators across 30 floors. Passengers from three social classes spawn with varying priorities:
- **EXECUTIVES (Magenta)**: Corporate elite. Extremely impatient. High reward (20 CR) but heavy capacity impact (2.0 units).
- **WORKERS (Cyan)**: Middle class. Moderate patience. Standard rewards (10 CR).
- **DRONES (Acid Green)**: Low-class service units. Patient but low reward.

## 🎨 High-Fidelity Aesthetic (v2.1)
- **Cinematic Office Backdrop**: The elevator shafts are now set against a high-resolution dystopian office lobby, featuring rain-slicked windows and neon-noir lighting.
- **Low-Poly Character Sprites**: Replaced abstract passenger dots with thematic 2D "spirit" avatars for Executives, Workers, and Drones.
- **Dynamic Manifest**: Watch as character sprites board and fill the elevator cars in real-time.

## 🎮 Dual Operation Modes
- **🕹️ MANUAL_CONTROL**: Direct hands-on management. Use the floor strip to call elevators and the HUD to dispatch units. Click cars for manual overrides.
- **💻 LOGISTICS_CODING**: Programmatic optimization. Define `init(elevators, floors)` and `update(dt, elevators, floors)` to automate your fleet.

## 💰 Scoring & Progression
- **Streak Multipliers**: Deliver units in rapid succession to build multipliers.
- **Infrastructure Shop**: Spend CR on permanent upgrades like Speed, Capacity, Fleet Expansion, and VIP Priority.
